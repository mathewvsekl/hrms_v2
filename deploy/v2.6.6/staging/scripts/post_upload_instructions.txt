# HRMS v2.6.6 Post-Upload Instructions (DirectAdmin)

1. UPLOAD
   - Upload 'HRMS_V2_v2.1.0_FULL.zip' one level ABOVE your public_html folders.

2. EXTRACT
   - Extract the ZIP. This will populate:
     - public_html/ (Main source)
     - database/ (Migration scripts)
     - scripts/ (Deployment guides)
     - storage/ (Internal files)
     - .env.example
     - VERSION

3. INITIALIZE DATABASE
   - Via phpMyAdmin, import 'database/schema.sql' then 'database/seed.sql'.

4. SETUP config/config.php
   - In 'public_html/config/', rename 'config.template.php' to 'config.php'.
   - Update database and SMTP credentials.

5. VERIFY
   - Access your domain to log in.
